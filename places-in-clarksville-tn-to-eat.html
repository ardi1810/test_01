The Best Restaurants in Clarksville

Clarksville, TN is a hidden gem in the heart of Tennessee. It is home to Fort Campbell and Austin Peay State University, and has a rich history that dates back to the Civil War. But what really sets Clarksville apart is its food scene. From Southern comfort food to international cuisine, Clarksville has it all. Here are some of the best places to eat in Clarksville.

1. Blackhorse Pub & Brewery

Blackhorse Pub & Brewery is a local favorite that has been serving up delicious food and brews since 1995. Their menu features classic pub fare like burgers, sandwiches, and pizzas, but with a Southern twist. Their beer selection is also impressive, with a variety of craft beers on tap. Don't miss their signature dish, the Blackhorse Ale Steak.

2. Strawberry Alley Ale Works

Strawberry Alley Ale Works is another popular spot in Clarksville. Located in a historic building in downtown Clarksville, this brewery and restaurant serves up seasonal and locally-sourced dishes. Their menu changes frequently, but you can always expect fresh and creative dishes like smoked pork belly and grilled octopus.

3. The Choppin' Block

If you're looking for a steakhouse, The Choppin' Block is the place to go. This upscale restaurant features prime cuts of beef, as well as seafood and pasta dishes. The atmosphere is elegant and sophisticated, making it the perfect spot for a special occasion.

4. The Catfish House

For classic Southern comfort food, head to The Catfish House. This family-owned restaurant has been serving up fried catfish, hushpuppies, and other Southern staples since 1971. The portions are generous and the prices are reasonable, making it a great spot for a casual dinner with friends or family.

5. El Bracero Mexican Restaurant

If you're in the mood for Mexican food, El Bracero is the place to go. This family-owned restaurant has been serving up authentic Mexican dishes for over 20 years. Their menu features classics like tacos and enchiladas, as well as more unique dishes like camarones a la diabla (spicy shrimp).

Other Places to Eat in Clarksville

6. The Gilroy Neighborhood Pub

The Gilroy Neighborhood Pub is a cozy spot that serves up craft beers and pub fare. Their menu features classic dishes like fish and chips, as well as unique offerings like a pimento cheese burger.

7. Edward's Steakhouse

Another great steakhouse in Clarksville is Edward's Steakhouse. This family-owned restaurant has been serving up steaks and seafood since 2007. Their menu features prime cuts of beef, as well as fresh seafood like lobster and scallops.

8. Sango Sushi

If you're in the mood for sushi, head to Sango Sushi. This Japanese restaurant serves up fresh and creative sushi rolls, as well as other Japanese dishes like ramen and udon noodles.

9. Nicoletta's Italian Cafe

For Italian food, Nicoletta's Italian Cafe is a must-visit. This cozy restaurant serves up classic Italian dishes like lasagna and chicken parmesan, as well as more unique offerings like pumpkin ravioli.

10. The Looking Glass Restaurant

Finally, The Looking Glass Restaurant is a unique spot that's worth checking out. This vegetarian and vegan restaurant serves up creative and delicious dishes like jackfruit tacos and vegan mac and cheese.

Conclusion

With so many great restaurants to choose from, it's hard to go wrong in Clarksville. Whether you're in the mood for Southern comfort food, international cuisine, or something in between, Clarksville has it all. So next time you're in town, be sure to check out some of these amazing places to eat.
