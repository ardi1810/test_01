Job Openings For Omaha Public Schools In 2023

Introduction

Omaha Public Schools (OPS) is one of the largest school districts in Nebraska, serving over 53,000 students across 87 schools. As the district continues to grow, so do the job opportunities for educators and staff. In this article, we will explore the job openings available in 2023 for OPS and provide tips on how to apply.

Teaching Positions

OPS is always on the lookout for dedicated and passionate teachers to join their team. In 2023, the district will be hiring for various teaching positions, including elementary, middle, and high school educators. To apply, candidates must have a valid teaching certificate and a degree in education or a related field. Additionally, experience working with diverse student populations is preferred.

Administrative Positions

In addition to teaching positions, OPS will also be hiring for various administrative roles in 2023. These positions include principals, assistant principals, and district-level administrators. Candidates must have a master’s degree in education or a related field, as well as experience in educational leadership. Strong communication, organizational, and problem-solving skills are also required.

Support Staff Positions

OPS also has a variety of support staff positions available in 2023, including custodians, bus drivers, and food service workers. These positions do not typically require a degree, but candidates must have a high school diploma or equivalent. Additionally, some positions may require a valid driver’s license and a clean driving record.

Tips for Applying

When applying for a job at OPS, it is important to tailor your application to the specific position and highlight your relevant experience. Be sure to read the job description carefully and include specific examples of how your skills and experience align with the requirements. Additionally, be sure to proofread your application for spelling and grammar errors.

Benefits of Working at OPS

Working at OPS comes with many benefits, including a competitive salary, health insurance, and retirement benefits. Additionally, the district offers professional development opportunities and a supportive work environment. OPS is committed to creating a diverse and inclusive workplace, and values the contributions of all employees.

Conclusion

If you are passionate about education and want to make a difference in the lives of students, consider applying for a job at Omaha Public Schools. With a variety of positions available in 2023, there is sure to be a role that aligns with your skills and experience. Remember to tailor your application and highlight your relevant experience, and take advantage of the many benefits that come with working at OPS.
