Introduction

The Independent Electoral and Boundaries Commission (IEBC) is a constitutional body mandated to conduct and supervise all public elections and referenda in Kenya. The commission is responsible for ensuring that the electoral process is free, fair, transparent, and credible. In this article, we will discuss the IEBC jobs shortlisted candidates 2022 Kenya and what it means for the commission.

The IEBC advertised for various positions in 2022, and the application process was open to all eligible Kenyans. The commission received thousands of applications for the advertised positions, and a rigorous shortlisting process was conducted to select the most qualified candidates for the jobs. The shortlisted candidates were then invited for interviews, and the successful ones will be offered employment.

The Shortlisting Process

The IEBC shortlisting process was thorough and aimed at identifying the most qualified candidates for the advertised positions. The commission used a combination of academic qualifications, work experience, and other relevant factors to shortlist the applicants. The shortlisted candidates were then invited for interviews, and their performance in the interviews will determine if they will be offered employment.

The Significance of the Shortlisting

The shortlisting process is significant to the IEBC as it ensures that only the most qualified candidates are employed in the commission. The shortlisted candidates have gone through a rigorous process, and their qualifications and experience have been thoroughly scrutinized. This ensures that the commission has a competent workforce that can deliver on its mandate.
