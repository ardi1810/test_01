What is Ulta Beauty?

Ulta Beauty is a beauty retailer that sells makeup, skincare, haircare, and fragrance products. They also offer salon services, such as haircuts, coloring, and styling. Ulta Beauty has both high-end and drugstore beauty products, making it a one-stop-shop for all your beauty needs.

Ulta Beauty offers a variety of part-time positions, including sales associates, beauty advisors, and salon associates.
