Valley Fair Mall, located in the heart of the Silicon Valley, is one of the most popular shopping destinations in California. Valley Fair Mall is home to many well-known retail brands, such as Macy's, Nordstrom, and Apple. Valley Fair Mall has a diverse selection of restaurants and cafes, ranging from fast food to fine dining.
