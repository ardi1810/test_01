Discover The Best Places To Eat In Goldsboro, Nc In 2023

Introduction

Goldsboro, North Carolina, is a small town that takes pride in its unique blend of southern charm and modern amenities. The town has a rich history, and its dining scene reflects that history. In this article, we will explore some of the best places to eat in Goldsboro, NC, in 2023.

The Flying Shamrock

The Flying Shamrock is one of Goldsboro's hidden gems. This Irish pub offers a cozy atmosphere, friendly staff, and delicious food. Their menu features traditional Irish dishes such as bangers and mash, shepherd's pie, and fish and chips. The pub also offers a wide selection of beers, including Irish and local craft brews.

Something Different

If you're looking for something different, head over to Something Different. This unique restaurant offers a fusion of southern and Asian cuisine. Their menu features dishes such as Korean BBQ tacos, shrimp and grits, and sushi rolls. The restaurant also offers a variety of craft cocktails and a cozy atmosphere.

Wilber's Barbecue

No trip to Goldsboro is complete without trying the town's famous barbecue. Wilber's Barbecue is a local institution that has been serving up delicious pork barbecue since 1962. The restaurant's traditional eastern North Carolina-style barbecue is slow-cooked over wood for a delicious smoky flavor. Be sure to try their famous hushpuppies and banana pudding.

Brewmasters

Brewmasters is Goldsboro's premier craft beer bar and restaurant. The restaurant offers a rotating selection of over 50 craft beers on tap and a menu featuring delicious pub fare. Their menu features dishes such as burgers, sandwiches, and wings. The restaurant also hosts regular events, including beer tastings and trivia nights.

The Brown Bag Cafe

The Brown Bag Cafe is a quaint cafe that offers delicious homemade sandwiches, salads, and soups. The cafe's cozy atmosphere and friendly staff make it the perfect spot for a quick lunch or a leisurely meal. Be sure to try their homemade chicken salad, it's a local favorite.

The Village Steakhouse

If you're in the mood for a steak, head over to The Village Steakhouse. This upscale restaurant offers a variety of steaks, seafood, and pasta dishes. Their menu also features a selection of local wines and craft beers. The restaurant's cozy atmosphere and friendly staff make it the perfect spot for a special occasion.

The Mad Boar

The Mad Boar is Goldsboro's premier hotel and restaurant. The restaurant offers a variety of dishes, including seafood, steaks, and pasta. The restaurant also features a selection of craft cocktails and local beers. Be sure to try their famous fried green tomatoes and shrimp and grits.

The Chicken Barn

The Chicken Barn is a local favorite that has been serving up delicious fried chicken since 1964. The restaurant's crispy and juicy chicken is a must-try. The Chicken Barn also serves up delicious sides, including mac and cheese, coleslaw, and hushpuppies.

The Blueberry Place

If you have a sweet tooth, be sure to head over to The Blueberry Place. This charming bakery offers a variety of delicious baked goods, including muffins, scones, and cakes. Be sure to try their famous blueberry pie, it's a local favorite.

Conclusion

Goldsboro, NC, may be a small town, but its dining scene is big on flavor. From traditional southern barbecue to unique fusion cuisine, Goldsboro has something to offer every palate. Be sure to check out these ten delicious spots on your next visit to Goldsboro.
